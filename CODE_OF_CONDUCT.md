# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Treat collaborators with respect. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or bad-faith disruption is not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still holding technical work to a high bar.
- Keep discussions focused on the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment or discrimination
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or review processes

## Enforcement

The repository owner may remove comments, restrict participation, or block contributors who violate these standards.
